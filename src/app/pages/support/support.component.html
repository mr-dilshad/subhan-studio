Support Page

At Subhan Studios, we are committed to providing exceptional support to ensure the success of your IT projects. Whether you need assistance with cloud migration, cybersecurity, digital infrastructure, or custom software solutions, our dedicated support team is here to help.

How Can We Help You?

We offer a variety of support services designed to meet your needs:

1. Technical Assistance

Having issues with one of our implemented solutions or need help troubleshooting? Our technical experts are available to provide fast and effective support. We specialize in:

  • System troubleshooting and bug fixes
  • Software performance optimization
  • Cloud platform management and maintenance
  • Network and infrastructure support
  • Integration assistance for APIs and third-party services

2. Project Queries and Guidance

If you have questions related to ongoing or completed projects, we are here to provide clear answers and guidance. Our team can assist you with:

  • Updates on your project’s progress
  • Post-deployment instructions and advice
  • Best practices for maximizing system performance
  • Help with future upgrades or feature additions

3. Consultation and Advisory

Looking for strategic advice on your IT infrastructure or future digital transformation projects? We offer personalized consultations to help you make informed decisions about:

  • Scaling and optimizing your digital environment
  • Choosing the right technologies for your business
  • Enhancing cybersecurity and data protection
  • Improving efficiency through automation and integration

How to Reach Us

We offer multiple ways to get the support you need:

  • Email Support: Reach us anytime at support@subhan-studios.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
  • Phone Support: Speak directly with our support team for urgent issues. Call us at +91 9891747557 between 9 AM - 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
  • Live Chat: For real-time assistance, use our live chat feature available on our website. Our team is ready to help with any queries or technical challenges.
  • Knowledge Base: Visit our Knowledge Base for self-help resources, including guides, FAQs, and troubleshooting tips.

Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

We provide SLAs to ensure that your business receives timely and professional support. Depending on the complexity and urgency of your issue, we offer:

  • Critical Issue Response: Immediate response and resolution within 1-2 hours for mission-critical issues.
  • Standard Support: Responses within 24 hours for general inquiries and non-critical issues.

Remote Support Options

If needed, our technical team can remotely access your systems (with your consent) to provide quick and direct support. We ensure full security and confidentiality throughout the process.

For any additional assistance or inquiries, don’t hesitate to reach out. We’re here to help you get the most from your IT solutions!
